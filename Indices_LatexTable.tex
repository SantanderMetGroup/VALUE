The columns `M', `T' and `E' indicate if the indices analyze marginal \protect\cite{Gutierrez_et_al_VALUE_2019}, temporal \protect\cite{Maraun_et_al_VALUE_2019} or extreme \protect\cite{Hertig_et_al_VALUE_2019} aspects, respectively. Highlighted in boldface those used in the synthesis VALUE paper \protect\cite{VALUE_Synthesis_2019}. Several standard indices defined in \protect\cite{ETCCDI_1999} have been also included in the table.
